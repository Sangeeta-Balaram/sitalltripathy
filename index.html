My Story

I was introduced to energetic sciences from a young age.

I grew up in a family where palm reading and astrology were practiced with discipline and respect. My grandfather read palms. His father was a professional astrologer. Conversations about timing, patterns, and life cycles were normal in my childhood.

As I grew older, I explored modern education and built my career in mind-body disciplines. I studied fitness, nervous system regulation, coaching, and structured wellness systems.

"Energy affects clarity. Clarity affects decisions. Decisions shape life direction."

Over time, I realized something important. Instead of separating ancient knowledge and modern learning, I chose to integrate them in a structured way.

Today, I use practical tools like numerology, tarot, and vaastu as clarity frameworks, not for prediction, but for alignment. This work forms the Energy pillar of a larger Mind–Body–Energy ecosystem I am building.
